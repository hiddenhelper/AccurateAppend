Add verified deliverable email addresses to your NationBuilder lists.

What you need to know

  • Fields required for AccuSend Email Append are: first name, last name, U.S. street address, city, state, and zip code.
  • If there is an email address present in the "Email - Primary" field, there will not be an email address added to that record.
  • The tag 'VERIFIEDEMAIL' will be added to each record where an email address has been added.
