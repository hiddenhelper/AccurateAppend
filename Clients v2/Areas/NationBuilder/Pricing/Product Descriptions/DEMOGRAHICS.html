Enhance your list by adding demographic information which may include: ethnicity, gender, age, income level, wealth, whether children are present in the household, homeowner type, length at residence, median home value, marital status, estimated income, and education.

What you need to know

  • Requires first and last name, U.S. street address, city, state, and zip code.
  • All results appear as tags within your nation except gender and birth date which will be changed in the appropriate fields.
  • The tag 'ChildrenPresent' will be added to records where children are present in the household.
  • The tag 'Ethnicity: originating ethnicity will be added to records to denote the individual's ethnicity.
  • The tag 'ESTWEALTH: range in US dollars' which denotes the individual's estimated wealth.
  • The tag 'HeadOfHousehold' will be added to individuals that are the head of household.
  • The tag 'Income: range in US dollars' will be added to records to denote an individual's estimated annual income.
  • The tag 'Married' will be added to individuals that are married.
  • The tag 'Divorced' will be added to individuals that are divorced.
  • The tag 'Single' will be added to individuals that are single.
  • The tag 'MedHomeValue: range in US dollars' will be added to denote an individual's median home value.
  • The tag 'Owner' will be added to individuals that own their residence.
  • The tag 'Renter' will be added to individuals that rent their residence.
  • The tag 'Residence: range in years' will be added to denote how long an individual has resided at their residence.
  • The tag 'School: highest level of education attended will be added to denote the highest level of education that an individual attended.
  • Gender will be added to the 'Gender' field.
  • Date of birth will be added to the 'Birth Date' field.
