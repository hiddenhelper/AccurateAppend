Add U.S. mobile phone numbers to yourNationBuilder lists with mobile phone append.

What you need to know

  • Fields necessary for mobile phone append are: first name, last name, U.S. street address, city, state, and zip code.
  • Existing phone numbers in the "MOBILE NUMBER" field will be overwritten where a new phone number is matched.
  • Newly added mobile phone numbers will be inserted into the "MOBILE NUMBER" field.
  • The tag 'PHONE APPENDED' will be added to each record where a phone number was appended.
