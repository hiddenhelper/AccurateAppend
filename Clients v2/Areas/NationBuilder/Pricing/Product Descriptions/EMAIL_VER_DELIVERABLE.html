Improve email deliverability and reduce bounces by identifying non-existent, invalid or expired email addresses with advanced verification technology. It works by contacting the email host's server and determining whether the address is active and receiving mail.

What you need to know

  • The only required field is "EMAIL" or "Email - Primary".
  • Email addresses contained in your NationBuilder list under the field "EMAIL" or "EMAIL - PRIMARY" and submitted through Accurate Append's application will have their delivery status verified in real-time.
  • The tag 'VERIFIEDEMAIL' will be added to each record for email addresses that have been verified as deliverable.
  • The tag 'UNVERIFIEDEMAIL' will be added to each record for email addresses that have been verified as not deliverable.
  • The tag 'UNABLETOVERIFYEMAIL' will be added to each record for email addresses that are not able to be verified.
