Add high quality U.S. landline numbers to your NationBuilder lists with AccuConnect phone append.

What you need to know

  • Fields necessary for AccuConnect landline phone append are: first name, last name, U.S. street address, U.S. city, U.S. state, and U.S. zip code
  • Existing phone numbers in the "HOME PHONE" field will be overwritten where a new phone number is matched.
  • Newly added landline phone numbers will be inserted into the "HOME PHONE" field.
  • The tag 'PHONE APPENDED' will be added to each record where a phone number was appended.
