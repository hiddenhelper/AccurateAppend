Validate and standardize your existing U.S. postal addresses. Address validation and standardization also includes correction on any common spelling errors with street name, city, state, and zip where possible.

What you need to know

  • Requires U.S. addresses.
  • Requires house number, street name, city, state, and zip code.
  • The tag 'CASS: Standardized' will be added to each record where the address was able to be successfully standardized.
  • The tag 'CASS: UnableToStandardize' will be added to each record where the address was unsuccessful in standardizing.
