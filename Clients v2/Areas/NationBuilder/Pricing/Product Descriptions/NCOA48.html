Update your U.S. postal addresses records for individuals who have filed the National Change of Address paperwork with the United States Postal Service.

What you need to know

  • Fields required for processing are: first name, last name, U.S. street address, city, state, and zip code.
  • The tag 'NCOA MoveDate: [date]' will be added to each record where a match has occurred; denoting the date the move was filed with the United States Postal Service.
  • The tag 'NCOA MoveType: [individual or family]' will be added to each record where a match has occurred; denoting the type of move which was filed with the United States Postal Service.
