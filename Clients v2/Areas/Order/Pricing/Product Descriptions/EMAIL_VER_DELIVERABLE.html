Email verification will help determine the delivery status of your existing email addresses in real-time by pinging the hosted email server to verify whether the mailbox is active and receiving mail at that time.

How It Works

  • Upload your file
  • Our system will ping the hosted email server for each existing email addresses as well as scan for bots, spam traps, etc. Emails that successfully pass both steps receive a deliverable status.
  • Each record should contain an email address. Records that do not contain an email address will be ignored.
  • Output will contain delivery status of Deliverable, Undeliverable, or Unknown. You will not be billed for Unknown responses.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How much does it cost?

You are billed for matches only and the cost per match varies depending on the size of your file. When you upload your file, our system will analyze your file and present estimates based on historical system-wide match rates and the size of your file.

Q: How long does it take?

You may receive results in a matter of minutes after submitting your file for processing, depending on the size of your file. 90% of orders are completed the same business day.

Q: How many records will you match?

The average match rate is 95%. For example: if you submit 100 records that contain name and address, then you can expect to receive 95% email statuses.
