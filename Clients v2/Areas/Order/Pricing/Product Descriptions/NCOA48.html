Update your records U.S. postal address for individuals who have filed the National Change of Address paperwork with the United States Postal Service.

What you need to know

  • Updating your records U.S. postal address for individuals who have filed the NCOA paperwork with the USPS provides the most up to date address information that correspond to the individual and address combination on your list.
  • Required Input Fields: first name, last name, (full name may be used in place of first and last), U.S. street address, U.S. city, U.S. state, and U.S. zip code.
  • Appended (Output) Fields: oaddress [output address], ocity [output city], ostate [output state], ozip [output zip], move type and move date where a match is present.
