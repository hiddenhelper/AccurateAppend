Add U.S. landline phone numbers to your list.

What you need to know

  • Our system will utilize a combination of your record's name and address to query our daily updated DA (directory assistance/411) landline database as well as our Premium landline database which is similar to DA (411) landline but with a household overlay to enhance matching capabilities.
  • Required Input Fields: first name, last name, (full name may be used in place of first and last), U.S. street address, U.S. city, U.S. state, and U.S. zip code.
  • Appended (Output) Fields: phone number and match type.
