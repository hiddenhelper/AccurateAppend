Validate existing phone numbers to determine the original line type as issued by the North American Numbering Plan Association (NANPA).

What you need to know

  • Phone validation will validate your input phone numbers in a single column as valid 10-digit phone numbers and determine the original line type as provisioned by NANPA.
  • Each record should contain U.S. or Canadian phone number and zip code. Records that do not contain a phone number and postal code will be ignored.
  • Output will include line type, area code, city, country code, FIPS, MSA, latitude, longitude, time zone, and status codes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How much does it cost?

You are billed for matches only and the cost per match varies depending on the size of your file. When you upload your file, our system will analyze your file and present estimates based on historical system-wide match rates and the size of your file.

Q: How long does it take?

You may receive results in a matter of minutes after submitting your file for processing, depending on the size of your file. 90% of orders are completed the same business day.

Q: How many records will you match?

The average match rate is 95%. For example: if you submit 100 records that contain phone number, then you can expect to receive 95% statuses.
